Good Vibes Only was born from a simple realization: the right words at the right time can change everything. One morning, a single quote shifted an entire perspective — and the idea took hold.

THE PROCESS

How We Curate

Not all quotes are created equal. Here's our process.

01

Discovery

We scour books, speeches, interviews, and timeless wisdom across cultures, eras, and disciplines. If it resonates, we find it.

02

Vetting

Every quote passes through our criteria: Is it authentic? Is it actionable? Does it have the power to shift perspective? If not, it doesn't make the cut.

03

Categorization

We tag each quote by category, mood, and use-case so you can find exactly what you need — whether it's Monday motivation or Friday gratitude.

04

Delivery

Our algorithm selects a deterministic daily quote — meaning everyone sees the same quote each day. It creates a shared moment of positivity across our community.
